About Me

Let me tell you a little about myself and what I do and like...

Producing Quality Products

I would like to start off by giving you a quick backstory, not like a spiderman backstory (I wish) a much more boring one. Before this I was in the food and beverage industry for 15 years and out of those 8 were in management. I decided to change fields when Covid it. During the lockdown, while people were fighting for toilet paper, I started playing with Photoshop and fell in love (don't tell my wife) I enrolled back in school and decided to pursue a degree in graphic design and web development. I am a freelance designer/web developer and now spend my time learning new concepts and applying them to get better in my new craft. On my free time I like making digital art, spending time with family, and taking landscape photography. Not going to lie, I was scared getting into a new career, especially a competitive one like design/web dev, but you got to follow your dreams to find your happiness. Well I am done boring you now, enjoy the site, and catch you on the flip side.
